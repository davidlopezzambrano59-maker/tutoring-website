Founder & Lead Tutor

David Lopez Zambrano

Physics Education Specialist

I am a Physics graduate from King’s College London, where I achieved a high 2:1 BSc in Physics (2025). Over the past four years, I have tutored A-Level Physics (primarily AQA, as well as Edexcel) and GCSE Maths and Physics, supporting more than 100 students in reaching their academic goals. Many of my students have achieved top grades and secured places at Russell Group universities, including Oxford and Cambridge. My teaching approach is highly individualised. I begin by understanding how each student learns most effectively—whether through visual explanations, verbal reasoning, or real-world analogies—and tailor my lessons accordingly. I place strong emphasis on developing a deep conceptual understanding before applying this knowledge to exam-style and past paper questions. This structured method ensures that students not only master the theory but also gain the clarity, confidence, and problem-solving flexibility required to excel in their exams.

Our Mission

Transforming Physics Education

Inspire Understanding

Physics should inspire curiosity, not fear. At DLZ Tutors, we make even the most complex A-Level Physics concepts clear and approachable. Our lessons transform challenging theories into simple, logical ideas that students can confidently apply. Whether through one-to-one, small group, or larger group sessions, we create an engaging environment where every learner builds genuine understanding and long-term confidence in Physics.

Achieve Excellence

Every student has the potential to excel with the right support. Our tailored A-Level Physics tutoring—covering all major exam boards—focuses on developing strong conceptual understanding and mastering exam-style questions. Using proven strategies, targeted past paper practice, and personalised feedback, we help students achieve top grades and gain entry into leading Russell Group universities, including Oxford and Cambridge.

Future Success

Our mission extends beyond exams. We prepare students for the demands of university-level physics and future STEM careers by strengthening analytical thinking, logical reasoning, and problem-solving skills. Whether learning individually or as part of a group, every student at DLZ Tutors gains the knowledge, confidence, and mindset to succeed in higher education and beyond.

What Drives Us

Passion for Physics

At DLZ Tutors, our passion for physics drives everything we do. We believe in the power of physics to explain and inspire understanding of the universe. This enthusiasm is contagious, motivating students to develop their own curiosity and lasting appreciation for the subject.

Student-Centered Approach

Every student learns differently. We take the time to understand individual learning styles and adapt our teaching methods accordingly—whether through one-to-one, small-group, or larger-group sessions. Our support doesn’t end at exams; we aim to nurture confidence and independence that last well into university and future STEM careers.
