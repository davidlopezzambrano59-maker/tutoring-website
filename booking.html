Frequently Asked

Booking Questions

How quickly can I start?

We can typically arrange your first session within 48-72 hours of your application, depending on your schedule and tutor availability.

What if I need to cancel?

We offer flexible cancellation with 24 hours notice. Emergency cancellations are handled case-by-case with full understanding.

Are online sessions as effective?

Absolutely! Our online platform includes interactive whiteboards, screen sharing, and all the tools needed for effective physics tutoring.

How do payments work?

We accept payments by bank transfer, card, or direct debit. Payment is typically required 24 hours before each session or monthly for regular students.

What materials do I need?

Just bring your textbooks, any specific questions, and a willingness to learn! We provide all additional resources and materials.

Can parents attend sessions?

Parents are welcome to sit in on sessions, especially for younger students. We also provide regular progress reports to keep parents informed.
